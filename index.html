General FAQs

Your Questions Answered

  • What types of clients do you transport?

    We provide secure, non-emergency transport for individuals with mental health conditions, behavioural challenges, or vulnerabilities that require specialist handling. Our services are used by local authorities, hospitals, care providers, and legal institutions.

  • Are your staff trained to manage challenging behaviour?

    Yes. Our team is fully trained in managing challenging behaviour, de-escalation techniques, and safeguarding protocols. We ensure each journey is handled with professionalism, patience, and respect.

  • Do you provide transport for court appearances or mental health tribunals?

    Absolutely. We offer reliable and discreet transport to court hearings, mental health tribunals, and legal appointments, ensuring clients arrive safely, on time, and with the appropriate escort

  • What makes your vehicles secure?

    Our vehicles are purpose-built or specially adapted for secure transport. They are fitted with privacy screens, GPS tracking, secure compartments, and restraint systems where necessary, to ensure the safety of both clients and staff.

  • Can transport be arranged at short notice?

    Yes, we understand that schedules can change. While we encourage advance bookings, we can accommodate short-notice or same-day requests depending on availability.
